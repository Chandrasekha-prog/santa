How It Works

  1. Each participant registers with their name and email address
  2. Once everyone has registered, the organizer clicks "Assign Secret Santas"
  3. The system randomly assigns each participant someone to gift
  4. Each participant receives an email with the name of their assigned person
  5. Keep your assigned person secret until gift exchange day!
